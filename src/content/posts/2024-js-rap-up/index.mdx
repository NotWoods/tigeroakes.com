## The Script

11 months into 2024... <br />
let's recap Javascript once more

### January

[iOS gets new browser engines](https://www.apple.com/newsroom/2024/01/apple-announces-changes-to-ios-safari-and-the-app-store-in-the-european-union/) <br />
Apple creates [PWA tension](https://www.theregister.com/2024/02/08/apple_web_apps_eu/)

### February

React Labs drops a [big update](https://react.dev/blog/2024/02/15/react-labs-what-we-have-been-working-on-february-2024) <br />
[Transferable buffers](https://github.com/tc39/proposal-arraybuffer-transfer) come out the gate

### March

[JSR](https://deno.com/blog/jsr_open_beta) comes alive <br />
World Wide Web [turns 35](https://webfoundation.org/2024/03/marking-the-webs-35th-birthday-an-open-letter/)

### April

Node 22 gives us [module `require()`](https://github.com/nodejs/node/pull/51977) <br />
[ESLint 9](https://eslint.org/docs/latest/use/migrate-to-9.0.0) sets configs on fire

### May

React 19 [enters RC](https://react.dev/blog/2024/04/25/react-19) <br />
[SolidStart 1](https://www.solidjs.com/blog/solid-start-the-shape-frameworks-to-come) adds simplicity

### June

This year's [spec is ratified](https://2ality.com/2024/06/ecmascript-2024.html) <br />
[JSNation](https://jsnation.com/2024/) on the EU side

### July

[Ladybird](https://ladybird.org/posts/announcement/) browser enters the race <br />
Node tries [type stripping](https://github.com/nodejs/node/pull/53725) whitespace

### August

[rspack](https://rspack.dev/blog/announcing-1-0) 1 hits 1.0 <br />
telling webpack you're too slow

### September

Tell Oracle: [drop JS trademark](https://javascript.tm/) <br />
So we can leave ECMAScript in the dark

### October

Here comes [NextJS 15](https://nextjs.org/blog/next-15) <br />
[Deno 2](https://deno.com/blog/v2.0), [Svelte 5](https://svelte.dev/blog/svelte-5-is-alive) - so fresh so clean

### November

Bluesky rising, Twitter's outcast <br />
[CSS gets a logo](https://github.com/CSS-Next/logo.css) at last

**JSNation will be a blast!**
