Bit Ball (Original)

Bit Ball was one of my first programs. It uses the Box2D physics engine to power the 20 physics-based puzzles in the game, which were based around timing the release of the ball correctly or moved elements of the stage around. Some crucial code later ceased to function, and I retired the game from the Google Play store. I may revisit it in the future with a recreation.
